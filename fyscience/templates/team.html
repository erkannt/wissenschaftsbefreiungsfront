The Team

We are Daniel & Lukas, two open science enthusiasts. Having worked in different positions of academia, from being an author to supporting institutions with grant writing and being part of the publisher side of things, we have come to appreciate the open and collaborative spirit of the academic community. Nonetheless, we came to acknowledge the constraints that still restrict the accessibility of scientific results.

Our goal is to foster accessibility and collaboration in the beautiful, globally networked academic communities. Also, we just can't stand when information that should be freely accessible is unnecessarily hidden behind paywalls.

Accordingly, our strategy is to:

  • create awareness about the potential of publisher's Open Access policies
  • help authors understand which research they can free from paywalls

Looking forward, we hope that research institutions, funders and other interested parties can use this tool to discover the potential that they have to drastically increase the amount of Open Access publications.

When we're not working on freeyourscience.org, Daniel is a tools and infrastructure software engineer and Lukas is a machine learning research engineer. Our activity here is linked in no way to these day jobs or our employers, and all our opinions are our own.
