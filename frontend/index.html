Liberate Science


More than 2.000.000 academic publications
can be made freely accessible today.


Publishers that keep papers behind a paywall often allow re-publishing the paper freely accessible for everyone in the world. However, many authors don't know of this opportunity or don't leverage it.

Find out which of your papers you are allowed to re-publish today:


This project is built on the datasets and API available by https://unpaywall.org/ as well as the API at https://v2.sherpa.ac.uk/api/ and is thereby dependent on the validity of the information collected at these services. Note, that the information presented on liberate.science is not legal advice or a legal guarantee. Specifically does it not relieve any author of the obligations originating from the contract(s) signed with respective publishers.
The number 2.000.000 in the header is a conservative estimate, determined via a data analysis pipeline that is publically available at https://github.com/erkannt/wissenschaftsbefreiungsfront
Should you be interested in an exchange, coorporation, specific features or support: please contact us at info@liberate.science
